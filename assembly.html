10. Assembly¶

10.1. Introduction¶

This documents describes the procedure for assembly of the three subunits that will become one unit of the DMS ready for installation. Objective is to build a single item ready as a simple reto-fit to fluid dispensers already in field use.

10.2. Optics Assembly¶

For this subassembly, first ensure you have all items as listed in Table 10.1 and the associated referenced tools or supplies. More complete details on each item, such as part number, vendor etc., is located in the master BOM maintained here 7818-Droplet SVN\trunk\Documents\7819 DMS Master BOM.xls, and it is mirrored here (for viewing only).
